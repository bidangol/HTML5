    • Who is Hiccup?

    Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III is main protagonist of the DreamWorks animated How to Train Your Dragon franchise. He is the son of Valka and the late Hooligan chief, Stoick the Vast, thus making him the heir, and later, the chief of the tribe.

    How old is Toothless?

    Once thought of as the “unholy offspring of lightning and death itself,” Toothless (20 in dragon years) has proven to be much more of a giant, winged pussycat than the stuff
    of nightmares. Playful, inquisitive, and intelligent, Toothless is more Hiccup's best friend than his pet.









    Who is Hiccup's father?

    Stoick the Vast was the chief of the Hooligan Tribe, the father of Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, and husband of Valka. He is 45 in the first film, and 50 in the second. He sacrifices himself to save Hiccup in the second film and dies. During Riders and Defenders of Berk, his dragon is a Thunderdrum named Thornado.









    How old is Hiccup?

    During the trilogy, Hiccup ages from 15 to 30; his visual coming-of-age is unprecedented in American animation. In a dramatic turn at the end of the first film, Hiccup loses part of his leg.
