Centilion

Centilion has 101 noses and 101 feet long. It has a wingspan of 303 feet, it has 2 eyes on 1 side. They have twice as more dangeres venom than a scauldron. They are snake like but has a wing like fin for swimming and they can fly a little bit by jumping and flaping the fins. They can live up to around 101 years and have up to 305 eggs.
