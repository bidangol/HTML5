    Web Design Steps

    • Determine the Intended Audience
      The colors, images, fonts, and layout should be tailored to the preferences of your audience. The type pf site content (reading level amount of animation, etc.) should be appropriate for your chosen audience.
    • Determine the Goals of the Site
      Some common goals of web sites are: to be informative, to create a personal home page, to to establish a corparate web presence, and to do business in e-commerce.
    • Sketch a Tentative page layout
      Be consistent in your overall layout—color, typefaces, logo location, navigation bar, etc. Plan your Home Page Layout to avoid scrolling. Plan your Content Page Layout to be consistent, with easy navigation.
